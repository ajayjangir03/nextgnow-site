About NextGNow

NextGNow is a modern telecom knowledge platform delivering structured learning resources across 5G, 6G, Open RAN, and AI-driven telecom networks.

Our mission is to simplify complex telecom technologies through clear explanations, real-world call flows, architecture diagrams, and continuous daily technical updates aligned with latest 3GPP releases.

The platform is designed for telecom engineers, researchers, students, and industry professionals who want to stay updated with next-generation wireless innovations.
